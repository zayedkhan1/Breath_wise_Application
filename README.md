# 🌬️ BreathWise – Guided Breathing Exercise

**BreathWise** is a single‑page, fully responsive web application that helps you reduce stress and improve focus through interactive, guided breathing exercises. With multiple breathing techniques, real‑time visual feedback, optional soundscapes, and progress tracking, it’s your personal calm‑in‑a‑browser.

👉 **Try it now** – https://zayedkhan1.github.io/Breath_wise_Application/

---

## ✨ Features

- **Smooth Breathing Animation**  
  A soft circle expands on "Inhale", holds, and contracts on "Exhale" – perfectly synced with your chosen rhythm.

- **5 Breathing Modes**  
  - **Box Breathing** (4‑4‑4‑4)  
  - **4‑7‑8 Breathing**  
  - **Relaxation Breathing** (5‑5)  
  - **Deep Focus Breathing** (6‑2‑6)  
  - **Custom Mode** – set your own inhale, hold, exhale durations and number of rounds.

- **Full Session Control**  
  Start, Pause, Resume, Stop, and Restart. See current phase, round count, remaining time, and total session duration at a glance.

- **Sound Guidance**  
  Choose from **Bell**, **Soft Breath**, **Nature**, **Ocean**, or **Rain** sounds – or mute everything. Phase‑change cues keep you on track.

- **Meditation Themes**  
  Switch between **Ocean**, **Forest**, **Night Sky**, **Minimal**, and **Zen Garden** – each with its own calming gradient and color palette.

- **Dark / Light Mode**  
  Toggle instantly. Your preference is saved.

- **Progress Dashboard**  
  Tracks total sessions completed, total breathing minutes, current streak, and longest streak – all stored locally in your browser.

- **Session Completion Screen**  
  Celebratory modal with rounds completed, time spent, and a motivational quote.

- **100% Mobile Responsive**  
  Works beautifully on phones, tablets, and desktops.

## 📖 How to Use

### 1. Choose Your Breathing Mode
- Click one of the mode tabs: **Box**, **4‑7‑8**, **Relax**, **Focus**, or **Custom**.
- For **Custom Mode**, adjust the sliders for Inhale, Hold, and Exhale durations, set the number of rounds, and click **Apply**. Your settings are saved automatically.

### 2. Start a Session
- Press the **▶️ (Start)** button or hit the **Spacebar**.
- The circle begins to expand and contract in sync with your chosen rhythm.
- The current phase (“Inhale”, “Hold”, “Exhale”) and remaining seconds are displayed inside the circle.
- A progress ring and phase dots help you track your position.

### 3. Control Your Session
- **⏸️ Pause** – temporarily halts the exercise.
- **▶️ Resume** – continues where you left off.
- **⏹️ Stop** – ends the session (you can start a new one).
- **⟳ Restart** – resets the session to the beginning.

### 4. Enhance with Sound
- Click any sound button (**Bell**, **Breath**, **Nature**, **Ocean**, **Rain**) to choose a background ambiance.
- A soft sound will play on each phase change (if not muted).
- Use the **🔊 / 🔇** button to mute/unmute all sounds.

### 5. Personalise Your Experience
- **Theme** – click the 🎨 button and select one of the five meditation themes.
- **Dark Mode** – click the 🌙 / ☀️ button to switch.

### 6. Track Your Progress
- The **Progress** card shows your cumulative stats:
  - **Sessions** – total completed sessions.
  - **Minutes** – total breathing minutes.
  - **Streak** – consecutive days with at least one session.
  - **Best** – longest streak ever.
- Click **Reset** if you want to clear your history (confirmation required).

### 7. Session Completion
- When you finish all rounds, a beautiful modal appears with your session summary.
- Click **Start New Session** to begin again.

### 8. Keyboard Shortcuts
- **Space** – Start / Pause / Resume (toggle).
- **S** – Stop.
- **R** – Restart.

## 🛠️ Installation & Setup

BreathWise is a single HTML file – no dependencies, no package managers, no build steps.

1. **Download** `index.html` from this repository.
2. **Open** it in any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).
3. All data (settings, progress, preferences) is stored locally in your browser’s `localStorage` – nothing is sent to any server.

> 💡 For the best experience, use the latest version of your browser.
